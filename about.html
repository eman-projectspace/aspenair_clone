Aspen Air - Our Story

Aspen Air is an exciting new heating and cooling business that is proudly Australian owned. With more than 30 years of experience in residential and commercial installations, we are able to provide a variety of heating and cooling solutions that best suit your needs.

Dedication and commitment have established Aspen Air as one of Melbourne’s highest regarded heating and cooling companies. Strong recognition throughout the industry has allowed us to have great relationships with leading brands, including Braemar, Brivis, Bonaire, Vulcan, Daikin, Panasonic, Fujitsu, Samsung, and LG brands.

Aspen Air offer uncompromised quality of service, a superior product range, competitive pricing and advice from trained professional staff. All this enables us to guarantee our systems provide maximum value and performance over the lifetime of your investment.
